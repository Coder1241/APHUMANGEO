Unit 5 Vocab
Gender Identity How a person sees themselves relating to gender.
Identity How people see themselves at different scales
Ethnocentrism When a culture believes that their culture is above the other culture.
Race A categorization of humans based on skin colors and other physical features.
Racism An attitude towards visible differences.
Residential segregation Two or more groups live separately from one another in different parts of an urban environment.
Invasion and Succession Process by which new immigrants to a city move to and dominate or take over areas or neighborhoods occupied by older immigrant groups.
Ethnicity An identity within a group of people bound by common culture.
Power Relationship The assumption and structures of who is in control and who has power over others.
Dowry The item to be given by the family of the Bride to the Father of the groom for marrying her.
Gender Inequality Index The measuring of labor-market participation, access to reproductive health, and empowerment.
Barrioization A dramatic increase in Hispanic population in a given neighborhood.
